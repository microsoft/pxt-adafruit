# Basic

Provides access to basic micro:bit functionality.

```cards
basic.showNumber(0);
basic.showLeds(`
. . . . .
. . . . .
. . # . .
. . . . .
. . . . .
`);
basic.showString("Hello!");
basic.clearScreen();
basic.forever(() => {
    
});
basic.pause(100);
basic.plotLeds(`
. . . . .
. . . . .
. . # . .
. . . . .
. . . . .
`);
basic.showAnimation(`
. . . . .
. . . . .
. . # . .
. . . . .
. . . . .
`);
```
